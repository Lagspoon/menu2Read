Menu2read

View project onGitHub

menu2Read is an app to present your menu on an iPad.

With menu2Read you can present to your customer all your food and drink. Data are stored both on the cloud and on your mobile device. Data are synchronized (API REST) automatically. Even if your network connection fail, your can present your menu to your customer.

You can edit your menu on the way with the Edition Menu app.
